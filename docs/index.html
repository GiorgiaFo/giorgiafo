    A little bit about us: Our family

    We are a family who moved to the US since 2015, and decided to open a restaurant together to bring a taste of our homeland, Sicily, to the people. This idea has brought two sisters and their families together, and we want to bring the best from us to you.

    And brought to life Sweet Sicily in Sunnyvale.

    • An authentic Italian business.
    • Great quality of food and drinks.
    • Vast selection of desserts and cookies.

    We have been and will keep putting lots of love and careful thought into all we do, with all of our great staff who has become part of our family. We hope you enjoy all we have to offer, and share the experience with others.

    Authetic Italian Pizza

    An authentic Italian pizza, a true culinary masterpiece that's made with high-quality ingredients and prepared with skill and care!

    One of the key ingredients that contribute to its unique texture and taste is the use of 00 flour,which makes it perfect for creating a light and crispy pizza crust Once the dough is ready, it is topped with fresh , imported ingredients such as our Italian salami, prosciutto, and much more.
